Star Wars Story Hologron: Conspiracy


An interesting idea that was explored in the sequel Star Wars trilogy is the return of the Galactic Empire,
also known as the First Order. The resemblance of both the Imperial Army and the First Order is remarkably
similar, from the armor, to even the name of the soldiers- Stormtroopers. Even the concept of Stormtrooper
designations are inherently the same.


The point of making is that there is a reason that these two are similar. It was planned. As we find out,
Emperor Palpatine created a Contingency Plan to rebuild the Empire should the Rebellion Alliance
manage to successfully disintegrate the organization. This is not the first time we’ve seen Palpatine’s
genius as we learn that he orchestrated the Clone Wars to rise in the political arena.


So here’s the juice of the proposal:

Throughout the prequel trilogies, the animated series, anthologies, and other spin-offs, the rise of the Empire
was hinted.It’s easier for viewers to catch it as we see the entire story at multiple perspectives. However,
these hints are almost always covered up, or dodged entirely much to the heroes’ chagrin. The was this one
point in Star Wars history where the whole plot was inches away from being exposed: the Clones themselves.
High ranking soldiers in the Grand Army of the Republic found evidence that they were programmed from
birth to be part of something more greater (more sinister, but they did not know that). However, Palpatine’s
forces quickly put the investigation to rest. A handful of characters in the universe became loose ends to the
plot, but were also shut down.


My story follows an old wanderer who has followed the plot from the start, and the timeline starts at the
collapse of the Empire. He created his own conspiracy theory. He’s an anti-Imperial, and a scrapper and was
aware of how suspicious the transition the Republic had to becoming an Empire. His first idea was that
Palpatine sabotaged the Republic from the inside. Of course, people thought he was just another lunatic.
In one of his scavenging trips, he finds an old Republic cruiser that was shot down as a result of Order 66,
(an executive order to mark and execute Jedi for treason). Mind you, the wanderer is a brilliant technology
expert. He revives a shutdown droid from the ship, and it glitches and accidentally opens up an encrypted
database full of the secrets that he knew, deep down, were true.


From there, he decides to embark on a long journey to try to make more sense of what was presented to him.
He felt like he was barely scratching the surface, and that there’s more to come. The Empire is coming back.

I wanted to create a fan story that serves an anthology that shows a grand conspiracy that spans throughout
all of Star Wars, and connect them all. This story is inspired by Star Wars: Clone Wars episode, “Conspiracy,”
and the Star Wars video game, Fallen Order.


The layout of the website will resemble a holocron. If not familiar, a holocron is a device that contains encrypted
information and can only be opened with the power of the Force. The wanderer does not know he’s a force
sensitive individual...yet. This website is a database of his thoughts, and his story on how his journey is going.
It will be like a captain’s log, or a journey entry. Within the entries are information he’s gathered that support his
claim on the greater conspiracy, and he asks the bigger questions no one is asking. My hope is to connect the dots
of the Star Wars conspiracy myself through this character.


Thank you for reading, and May the Force be with you.
